Info from second file. We say a picture is worth a thousand words. Pictures are an important aid to comprehension for students who are just beginning to learn English. In the 1st English Course reading activities, the sentences are short with some key vocabulary. The picture helps you with comprehension and to learn English words that might be new. You’ll hear the pronunciation of all the words on the screen when you click the Listen button. A good learning strategy is to listen to the speaker several times as you read along with the text.
